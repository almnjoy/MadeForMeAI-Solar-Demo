# NorthernPWR — Company Overview

## Who They Are

Northern PWR (NPWR) is a Canadian residential solar installer operating across Alberta, British Columbia, Manitoba, and Saskatchewan. They handle the full process: consultation, design, permitting, installation, and monitoring setup.
