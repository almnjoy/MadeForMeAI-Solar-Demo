# TrueNorth Solar — Install Process

Source: https://truenorth.madeformeai.com/how-it-works

## Step-by-Step Overview

### 1. Free Consultation
A solar advisor reviews the homeowner's energy usage (12 months of utility bills), roof layout, and location. No pressure — transparent and fact-based. AI-assisted scheduling means you can book online 24/7.

### 2. Custom System Design
Using satellite imagery and on-site survey:
- Assess roof dimensions, orientation, and optimal panel placement (typically south-facing)
- Size the system to match historical energy consumption
- Present final design showing panel layout and projected energy output

### 3. On-Site Survey
Physical inspection of the roof and main electrical panel to verify the satellite-based design. Ensures accuracy before permitting.

### 4. Permitting
TrueNorth handles permits with local authorities. Timeline varies by municipality.

### 5. Installation
Professional crew installs panels, racking, inverters, and wiring. Safety protocols followed throughout (PPE, OHS standards, site inspections).

### 6. Monitoring Setup
Customer is set up with a monitoring portal to track solar production, consumption, and receive alerts. TrueNorth's AI agents can answer monitoring questions 24/7.

### 7. Activation
System is activated and connected to the grid.

## What Homeowners Should Know

- Bring 12 months of electricity bills to the consultation — this drives system sizing
- Most of the process is handled by TrueNorth; homeowner action is minimal after signing
- Customers are kept informed at every stage via AI-powered updates and the team
- TrueNorth does not currently offer cleaning/maintenance services — contact a local contractor if needed
- TrueNorth guarantees the system produces at least 85% of the estimated annual energy in the contract

## Warranties

- Solar panels: 25-year performance warranty (through manufacturer)
- Other components: individual manufacturer warranties
- Installation workmanship: TrueNorth workmanship warranty included
